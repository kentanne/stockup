About Our System

The StockUp Inventory Management System helps educational institutions efficiently manage their inventory of school supplies and resources.

Quick Reference Guides

Navigating the Dashboard

Steps:
  • 1. Log in to the system using your username and password.
  • 2. Click on the Dashboard option from the navigation bar.
  • 3. View key information such as:
    • - Popular items
    • - Recent user activities
    • - Inventory summaries
  • 4. Use shortcuts on the dashboard to access specific features.

Adding New Items to Inventory

Steps:
  • 1. Navigate to the Inventory section.
  • 2. Click on the Add Item button.
  • 3. Fill in the required details such as:
    • - Item name
    • - Category
    • - Quantity
    • - Other specifications
  • 4. Click the Save button to add the item to the inventory.

Removing Items from Inventory

Steps:
  • 1. Go to the Inventory section.
  • 2. Select the item you wish to remove.
  • 3. Click the Delete button.
  • 4. Confirm the deletion to remove the item from the system.

Generating Stock Reports

Steps:
  • 1. Go to the Reports section from the dashboard.
  • 2. Choose the type of report you wish to generate.
  • 3. Set the filters such as date range, categories, or item status.
  • 4. Click on Generate Report to view or download the report.
